Cat causes chaos for local family

Written by Celia Roush

When the Roush adopted a kitten, they did not expect that this cat was a chaos gremlin. The Roush's found their cat when she was she was about a day old, She was so young,, Angie Roush reports, Her mother was no where to be seen, so we took her home. Little did they know, the cat would end up being a chaotic creature, causing problems way too often.

Brunhidle, Bruni for short, would end up scraching and biting everyone, stealing hair ties and hiding them under her cat tower, and begging for food ever ten minutes.

She often goes by the door to try and go outside, but when she's outside, she just flops on the ground and sleeps - Ginny Roush

The other Roush daughter reports that Bruni often likes to climb windows. She thinks she spiderman, or spidercat Will Bruni ever behave like a normal cat? Only time will tell
